Americans have caught green felt fever, and gambling is staking its claim as one of the country’s hottest new pastimes. Now, revealing the smarts, loyalty, and sheer love of the game that would draw a nod from Danny Ocean, Tom Breitling—a guy who had never seen The Godfather or a $100 bill until he went to college—tells how he and his best friend Tim Poster made their second $100 million in Vegas by age 35. DOUBLE OR NOTHING: How Two Friends Risked It All to Buy One of Las Vegas’ Legendary Casinos (Collins; March 18, 2008; $24.95) is an engrossing true story of friendship, fearless gamesmanship, trust, risk-taking, and how Tom and Tim, the youngest casino owners in Nevada, hit the jackpot in two industries.
